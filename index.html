Our Concept.

We have 6 goals regarding Waterpoint™.

Cheaply available

One of the main goals is to make Waterpoint™ accessible to under-developed nations.

Sustainable solution

The second goal is to make it sustainable and more competitive compared to other networks and 
water transportation systems.

Secure and Hygienic

The main advantage of using the Waterpoint™ system, is that it uses certified filtration systems to clean the naturally sourced (Aquifer-)water.

Organic materials

To meet our sustainability goal, we use generally available materials and eco-friendly plastics and chemicals.

Certified and approved

Our network is certified and approved by many industry standards, including the FAOs standard. (Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations) 

Simple and Modular

Our modular system allows a rage of installations requirements while maintaining simple operating and maintenance procedures.
